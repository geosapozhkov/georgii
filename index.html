This project is a lifelong concept. It consists of three parts: "Than", "Now", and "Then". The project "MIND" explores the human conflict between the irrational and the rational.

The collapse of the RCII serves as the event giving birth to "Then", "Now", and "Then". RCII was a huge, colossal laboratory representing humanity's rational designing and classification of our own life.

This project challenges the notion of totalitarianism and aims to find a balance between the rational and irrational aspects of human existence. Through this exploration, we seek to understand the complex nature of human consciousness and the structures that define our reality.

The three temporal states represent different phases of human development and understanding, each offering unique perspectives on the relationship between order and chaos, structure and freedom.
